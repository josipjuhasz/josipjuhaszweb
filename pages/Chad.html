Chad Smith

Chad Smith, (born 25 October 1961) is an American musician who has been the drummer of the band Red Hot Chili Peppers since 1988. The group was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2012. Smith is also the drummer of the hard rock supergroup Chickenfoot, formed in 2008, and of the all-instrumental outfit Chad Smith's Bombastic Meatbats, formed in 2007.

Some interesting facts

  • Spin magazine placed Smith at #10 on their list of the "100 Greatest Drummers of Alternative Music" in May 2013
  • Chad did not receive formal drum lessons, and gained drumming experience by playing in school bands.'
  • Smith ran away from home when he was 15, but returned home after a summer.
